What is a Naturopathic Clinic?

A naturopathic clinic focuses on natural and holistic approaches to healthcare. Unlike conventional medicine that treats symptoms, naturopathy emphasizes prevention, underlying causes, and patient-centered care. Licensed naturopaths at Inside Health Clinic use evidence-based natural treatments like herbal medicine, IV therapy, nutrition counseling, lifestyle interventions, and advanced lab testing to support the body's innate healing abilities.
